## 身上有刺的恐龍 / A Dinosaur with Spikes
科學家在中國發現了一種很特別的恐龍化石。這種恐龍叫作「豪龍」，它的名字意思是「多刺的大龍」。最神氣的地方是，它的背上和脖子上長滿了尖尖的、像刺蝟一樣的硬刺！

Scientists found a very special dinosaur fossil in China. It is called Haolong, which means "spiny dragon." The coolest part is that it had sharp spikes on its back and neck, just like a porcupine!

<!--more-->

## 恐龍界的「防彈衣」 / A Dino’s "Body Armor"
這些刺是中空的，長在恐龍的皮膚上。科學家覺得這些刺就像是它的盔甲，可以保護它不被壞壞的肉食恐龍吃掉。

These spikes were hollow and grew on its skin. Scientists think the spikes were like armor. They helped protect the dinosaur from being eaten by scary meat-eating dinosaurs.

## 它是個「草食小巨人」 / A Gentle Plant-Eater
雖然長得很有威嚴，但豪龍其實是吃草的。這次發現的是一隻「恐龍寶寶」，大約 2.4 公尺長，跟一輛小汽車差不多大。如果是長大的豪龍，可能會長到 5 公尺那麼長喔！

Even though it looked tough, Haolong actually ate plants. The fossil found was a "baby dinosaur." It was about 2.4 meters long, which is like a small car. Grown-up ones could be as long as 5 meters!

## 重要的發現 / An Important Discovery
這是科學家第一次在這種恐龍身上看到硬刺。這讓大家知道，原來恐龍的皮膚不只有鱗片或羽毛，還有更多不同的樣子。

This is the first time scientists have seen spikes on this kind of dinosaur. It shows us that dinosaur skin wasn't just scales or feathers—it could look many different ways!
