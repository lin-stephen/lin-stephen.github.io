## 發現了新奇的事！ / A Surprising Discovery!
在奧地利，有一隻名叫 Veronika 的寵物牛。最近，科學家發現她做了一件非常特別的事。

In Austria, there is a pet cow named Veronika. Recently, scientists found her doing something very special.

## 她會使用工具！ / She Can Use Tools!
Veronika 會用嘴巴叼起長柄刷子或木棍。當她覺得背上或是肚子癢癢，但手腳抓不到的時候，她就會用這些東西來幫自己抓癢。

Veronika uses her mouth to pick up a brush or a stick. When she feels itchy but cannot reach the spot, she uses these tools to scratch herself.

<!--more-->

## 她真的很聰明 / She Is Really Smart
最厲害的是，她知道刷子有兩頭。皮比較厚的地方，她會用硬硬的刷毛那頭；如果是肚子比較嫩的地方，她會換成平滑的木棍那頭，輕輕地推。

Most importantly, she knows the brush has two ends. For thicker skin, she uses the hard bristles. For softer skin, she switches to the smooth stick end.

## 科學家很驚訝 / Scientists Are Surprised
科學家以前覺得只有猴子或黑猩猩才會這樣聰明地使用工具。現在他們發現，原來牛也比我們想像中還要聰明很多呢！

Scientists used to think only monkeys or chimps could use tools like this. Now they know cows are much smarter than we thought!
